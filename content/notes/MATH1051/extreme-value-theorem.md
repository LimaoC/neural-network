Let $f$ be [continuous](notes/MATH1051/continuity.md) on a closed interval $[a, b]$. Then $f$ attains its global maxima and minima on $[a, b]$, and these values occur either at the endpoints $a$ and $b$ or a critical point inside $[a, b]$.
